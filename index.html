Beer Recommendation Engine using Text Analytics

There are several recommendation websites at present, where we type in what we desire or have a chat with a bot, and get recommendations tailored to the words we put. One of the key algorithms used for this purpose is looking at the reviews of all the products, taking keywords from the input you provided and matching the products based on the similarity of the reviews and your comment as well as the sentiment behind it. For example, if you said "I want a Fruity beer with good aroma", The engine will look for similarity between the reviews and association with the word "Fruity" and "aroma", then it will take the sentiments to see whether people have positive opinion about the attributes and based on that a results are shown. As a part of our course in Fall Semester, my group and I have tried to make such a product using Selenium, SpaCy similarity and Vader Sentiment packages.
